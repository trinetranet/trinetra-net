Legal coverage

Six laws.
Your complete protection.

Every clause is cross-referenced against these regulations with exact section citations and plain-English explanations of your rights.

RBI — India
RBI e-Mandate Circular 2021
Controls all recurring payments and auto-renewals in India.
Your right: Companies must send a pre-debit notification 24h before any recurring charge. Dispute via your bank if violated.
India
DPDP Act 2023
India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act — most comprehensive data law yet.
Your right: Explicit consent required for every data use. Request deletion within 72 hours under §12.
India
Consumer Protection Act 2019
Defines unfair contract terms and the right to approach consumer courts.
Your right: §100 guarantees consumer court access regardless of any arbitration clause.
EU
GDPR
Applies to any company serving users in the EU — fines up to €20M.
Your right: Right to erasure, portability, and knowing exactly what data is held and why.
India
Copyright Act 1957
Protects your ownership of content you create and post online.
Your right: §17 makes you the first owner. Platforms can only use your content under a license you specifically grant.
India
Indian Contract Act 1872
Governs when clauses in contracts can be declared void.
Your right: §23 voids clauses that are unconscionable, against public policy, or obtained under undue influence.
